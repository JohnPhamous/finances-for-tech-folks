## Build Credit

- Credit Score
- Metric used for loans and renting based on:
  - Paying your bills on time
  - How long you've had your credit lines
- Start by applying for credit cards

---

## Using a Credit Card

- Swap using cash/debit cards with a credit card
- Gain points that can be cashed out or converted to travel
  - Travel the world for free by spending what you would have otherwise!
- Pay off your bill on time
- Credit card perks:
  - Purchase protection
  - Extended warranties
  - Free TSA Pre-Check/Global Entry
  - Perks for renting cars and hotels
  - Special access to events
